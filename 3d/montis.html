Augmented Reality Product Interaction

Montis AR is an augmented reality application that allows potential customers to virtually try out Montis chairs in their home before buying. The customer prints a marker, places it on a location on the floor and opens the app on a phone or tablet. The app gives a live video feed of the room in addition to a high-polygon rendering of a Montis chair at the position of the printed marker. We used Unity3D, Qualcomm Vuforia for the AR and Scaleform for the user interface.
